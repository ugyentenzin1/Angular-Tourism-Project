About Bhutan

The Land Of Thunder Dragon

Bhutan, a hidden kingdom nestled in the towering Himalayan range, is a highly coveted destination that even seasoned travelers consider a privilege to visit. The region boasts of ancient sacred monasteries perched on sheer cliffs, colorful prayer flags lining mountain ridges, and red-robed monhanting in distant temples. During Buddhist festivals, vibrant masked dancers perform ancient rituals on sacred Dzong (fortress) grounds. The people of Bhutan have a rich cultural heritage that is deeply rooted in their timeless identity. Despite opening its doors to tourism in 1974, the number of visitors is kept to an environmentally sustainable level through government-regulated tariffs. Bhutan is a predominantly Buddhist kingdom, with its people renowned for their happiness and proud of their national costume. Gagel Tours and Travels offers a unique opportunity to explore the awe-inspiring mysteries of the land, from snow-clad mountains, lush valleys, dense forests, and rushing streams to hidden rituals in remote monasteries and the daily life in far-flung rural communities. Gagel Tours and Travels provides access to the dream Himalayan adventure.
